Privacy Policy for FluentasAI SMS Services
Effective Date: 10 July 2026
Last Updated: 10 July 2026

1. Introduction
MicroHealth LLC ("MicroHealth," "we," "us," or "our") operates the FluentasAI platform accessible at https://ai.microhealthllc.com/ (the "Website" or "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our SMS messaging capabilities, including our Send and SignFlow features, in compliance with A2P 10DLC (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code) regulations.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information
When you use our SMS services, we may collect:
●	Phone numbers (sender and recipient)
●	Name and contact information
●	Message content and metadata
●	Timestamps and delivery status
●	Device information and carrier details
●	IP addresses and usage data

2.2 SMS-Specific Data
For our Send and SignFlow SMS capabilities:
●	Opt-in/opt-out preferences
●	Message delivery confirmations
●	Campaign identifiers
●	Consent records and timestamps
●	Message templates and customizations

3. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information to:
●	Deliver SMS messages through our Send and SignFlow features
●	Maintain A2P 10DLC compliance and carrier requirements
●	Process message delivery and track status
●	Manage opt-in and opt-out requests
●	Prevent fraud and abuse
●	Improve our services and user experience
●	Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

4. A2P 10DLC Compliance

4.1 Consent Requirements
●	We obtain express written consent before sending SMS messages
●	Users can opt-out at any time by replying "STOP"
●	We maintain records of all consent and opt-out requests
●	We honor opt-out requests within the timeframe required by law

4.2 Message Content
●	We send only authorized message types registered with carriers
●	Messages clearly identify MicroHealth as the sender
●	We do not send prohibited content including spam, phishing, or illegal material
●	All campaigns are registered with The Campaign Registry (TCR)

4.3 Rate Limits and Throughput
●	We comply with carrier-imposed rate limits
●	Messages are sent according to approved use cases
●	We maintain appropriate message volume controls

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We may share your information with:

5.1 Service Providers
●	SMS gateway providers and telecommunications carriers
●	The Campaign Registry (TCR) for A2P 10DLC registration
●	Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
●	Analytics and monitoring services

5.2 Legal Requirements
●	When required by law, regulation, or legal process
●	To protect our rights, property, or safety
●	To prevent fraud or security threats
●	In connection with business transfers or acquisitions

5.3 Third Parties
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention
●	SMS message data is retained for 90 days unless longer retention is required
●	Opt-out records are maintained indefinitely to honor preferences
●	Consent records are kept for 3 years minimum
●	Compliance logs are retained per regulatory requirements
